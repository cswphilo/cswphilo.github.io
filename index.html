Research Interests

I am interested in structural proof theory and its connection with other disciplines.
In particular, I am now working on
  • characterizing proof systems for variants of skew monoidal categories,
  • constructing categorical models for variants of multiplicative intuitionistic linear logics,
  • formalization of aforementioned proof systems in Agda.
Keywords: Curry-Howard-Lambek correspondence, structural proof theory, intuitionistic logic, categorical logic, linear logic, skew monoidal category, Agda
